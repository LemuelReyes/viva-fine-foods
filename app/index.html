Welcome to Vibe

In the heart of Kitsilano

Vibe Cafe has been serving the Kitsilano community for more than a decade, serving scrumptious breakfasts, hearty lunches and delicious homemade pastries. Try our organic, always fresh, and fair-trade coffee or enjoy our Chai latte frappes just in time for the summer.

The legacy of Vibe Cafe dates back three generations, first originating in Italy and expanding to its new home in Kitsilano in 1999. Owned and run by local Mike Sorrentino, he has long since kept the tradition of perfecting the pure craft of coffee. Not to be outdone, this trained chef has pastries coming out by the hour and serves lunch that will complete your Sunday afternoon.

Vibe Cafe's brand has also reached its influence and presence in Toronto, California, and Paris. Vibe has always been about community and has fostered a long established culture of inclusion through crafts, arts and music. Open mics are regularly held and artists are always encouraged to share every weekend.

Drop by and say hello for some free coffee samplers, on us.
